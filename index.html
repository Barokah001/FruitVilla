Fruits in season

Avocado

Avocados are a powerhouse of nutrients and healthy fats. They are rich in monounsaturated fatty acids that help lower bad cholesterol and reduce the risk of heart disease. Avocados also contain potassium, even more than bananas, which helps regulate blood pressure.

Banana

Bananas are a naturally sweet, energy-rich fruit that offers a quick fuel boost thanks to their carbohydrate content. They are famously high in potassium, which supports heart health and helps prevent muscle cramps. Bananas also contain vitamin B6, which aids in brain development and function.

Orange

Known for their vibrant color and refreshing taste, oranges are bursting with vitamin C, a crucial antioxidant that strengthens the immune system and promotes collagen production for healthier skin. Oranges also provide folate, potassium, and thiamine, all essential for heart and metabolic health. Their rich flavonoid profile, especially hesperidin.
